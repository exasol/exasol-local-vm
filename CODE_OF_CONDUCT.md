# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, professional, and
constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat other people with respect.
- Keep discussions focused on the technical issue at hand.
- Give and receive feedback constructively.
- Be patient with new contributors.
- Disagree with ideas without attacking people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or repeated disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that makes the project unsafe or unwelcoming for contributors.

## Reporting

If you need to report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately
or email `infosec@exasol.com` when the issue is sensitive.

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation
when needed to keep the project productive and respectful.
